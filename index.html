Why it's addictive

Everything you need to go viral (or flop)

Simple to play, surprisingly deep. Here's what you get.

Post your ideas

Fire off a title and a punchy description into the Genius or Dumb world. Start with 3 idea slots per world.

Vote every day

10 votes per world, per day, refreshed automatically. Every tap fires a burst of confetti.

Trending that moves

Today, this week, this month or all-time. Hot ideas rise fast - keep an eye on the charts.

Leaderboards

Top-100 creators per world with a top-3 podium. Rack up votes and claim your spot.

Ranks to unlock

Seven tiers per world based on the votes you earn. Watch your rank badge climb.

Unlock more slots

Every idea that hits 100 votes earns you an extra idea slot. Big hits mean more room to create.

Browse as a guest

Scroll the feeds without an account. Sign in with Google or Apple when you're ready to play.

Safe by design

Report, block, and a profanity filter keep the feed fun. Objectionable content is a hard no.

Delightfully animated

Playful splash, glowing controls, gradient titles and smooth card animations throughout.
